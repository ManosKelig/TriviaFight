TriviaFight

TriviaFight is a fun game of answering questions!

1-player mode

Challenge yourself on single-player. If in the end you have more correct than incorrect answers, you win!

2-player mode

Go head-to-head with a friend. Simply choose 2-player mode, pick a category and you’re set. Each player will get different questions on the chosen topic. Whoever gets the most correct answers, wins!

Categories / Difficulty

You can pick the category and the difficulty you want right before the game starts. If the difficulty you choose is “Any”, you will get questions with randomly chosen difficulties. Similarly, if the category you choose is “Random”, you will get questions from random categories.

Options

You can change the names of the players as well as the number of questions in the game from the “Options” menu.
